# Product North Star

## One-line positioning

AI Process & Spec Workbench for business-to-software delivery.

## Vietnamese positioning

Nền tảng AI giúp chuyển tri thức nghiệp vụ thành quy trình, yêu cầu, user stories, solution spec, UI spec và AI-ready coding context có kiểm soát.

## What the product is

The product is a governed AI workbench for:
- process modeling
- requirement generation
- delivery artifact generation
- solution and architecture specification
- AI coding context export
- quality checking
- traceability

## What the product is not

The product is not only:
- a BPMN generator
- a BRD generator
- a document converter
- a generic chatbot
- a pure vibe coding tool

## Strategic thesis

As AI coding tools become stronger, users will need better business context, structured specs, quality gates, and traceability before handing work to Codex, Claude Code, Cursor, Copilot, or other AI development tools.

The product sits between business intent and AI-assisted software delivery.

## Tagline candidates

- From business intent to AI-ready delivery specs.
- From process to production, with traceability.
- Turn business knowledge into governed delivery artifacts.
- Build better AI coding context from real business requirements.
